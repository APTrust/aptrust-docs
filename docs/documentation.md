## Trusted Digital Repository (ISO 16363)

APTrust's self-certification against the TDR audit standard (ISO 16363) and the Open
Archival Information System reference model (OAIS / ISO 14721). This is the most
substantial section and covers three audit areas in detail:

- **Organizational Infrastructure** — mission, strategic planning, staffing, succession,
  governance, financial sustainability, and policy management
- **Digital Object Management** — ingest workflows, AIP/SIP/DIP construction, preservation
  planning, fixity, access, and metadata
- **Infrastructure & Security Risk Management** — risk identification, security controls,
  disaster planning, and incident response
